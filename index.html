Welcome to SideEye!

SideEye is the unofficial app store to sideload web apps onto your Meta Ray-Ban Displays.

When you copy an app's link from this site and send it to your phone, you can instantly access the app on your glasses.

Developer Mode Required

If you have Developer Mode enabled, you'll be one tap away from adding the Web App. Otherwise, you'll be asked to enable Developer Mode first.

How to enable Developer Mode:

  1. Open the Meta AI app on your paired iOS or Android device.
  2. Select Settings > App Info, and then tap the App version number five times to display a pop-up that enables Developer Mode.
  3. Click Enable to confirm.

Note: Developer Mode persists across sessions, so you don't need to re-enable it each time you open the Meta AI app.
